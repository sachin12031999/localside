Types of Ambulances

Standard Ambulance

The standard ambulance is equipped with basic medical equipment and staffed with trained medical personnel to provide emergency care during transport.

Advanced Life Support Ambulance

The advanced life support ambulance is equipped with advanced medical equipment and staffed with highly trained medical personnel to provide advanced emergency care during transport.

Bariatric Ambulance

The bariatric ambulance is specially equipped to accommodate individuals with obesity or weight-related medical conditions during transport.
